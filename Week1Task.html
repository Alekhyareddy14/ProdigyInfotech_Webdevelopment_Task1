Our Services

What internships are provided? ▶

We provide internships in various fields:

  • Web Development
  • Full Stack Web Development
  • Mobile App Development
  • Data Science
  • Machine Learning
How long are the internships? ▶

Every internship is for only one month.

  • Web Development: 1 month
  • Full Stack Web Development: 1 month
  • Mobile App Development: 1 month
  • Data Science: 1 month
  • Machine Learning: 1 month
Is it a paid internship or unpaid internship? ▶

It is an unpaid internship. It improves the skills of the fresher.

Is a certificate given after completion of the course? ▶

Yes, a certificate is given after completion of the course, which can be a valuable addition to your resume and help in your future career endeavors.

Can we do multiple internships at a time? ▶

No, only one internship is allowed at a time. If you want to do web development, you choose web development. Multiple internships are not possible.
